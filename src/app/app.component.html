The area between the Sun and Earth is not a static void. That environment is constantly changing: these variations are called space weather.

The National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) provides minute-by-minute data on space weather, although not during all times of day. The raw data used for this app is available here.

These graphs update every minute. Invalid data entries are omitted.
